When I saw you

I didn't even like you

I don't really know how it happened.

We would spend hours talking about the word and its secrets.

"Don't you think the Illuminati....or some shadow group run the world from behind the scenes?"

"Yes, but they don't control the world."

And we would go on and on.

Until the store told us to leave because they were closing.

I slowly fell in Love with the way you smiled.

The way your eyes lit up when you talked about you dream career.

You wanted to be a movie critic, right?

It was a risky job but who was I to speak?

I was the one in the silly liberal arts major.

And slowly over time it happened.

I couldn't at the time.

But if I could change anything

I would have told you I loved you.
